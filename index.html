Our Mission

In Canada, only 9% of plastic waste is actually recycled, while the rest go into landfills and the environment. In the United States, only 5% is actually recycled. Together we can change this and work to impact the environment in a positive way.

Recycl is an educational tool powered by computer vision AI. It uses an AI algorithm to help classify different types of recyclables and where they should go. Our aim is to reduce the amount of recyclables going into landfills.
